About Ibadurrahman College

Ibadurrahman College and Halqatu Ibadurrahman together form a premier institution dedicated to nurturing well-rounded individuals through a unique blend of Western education and Tahfiz & Islamic Studies.

Halqatu Ibadurrahman focuses on deepening students' understanding of the Quran and Islamic principles, fostering spiritual growth and moral integrity. Meanwhile, Ibadurrahman College provides a robust Western education, equipping students with academic skills to excel in a globalized world. Our mission is to create leaders who are grounded in faith and empowered by knowledge.

Sadiku Muhammad Ahmad

Director

"At Halqatu Ibadurrahman and Ibadurrahman College, we are committed to fostering academic excellence and spiritual growth, preparing students to lead with integrity and wisdom."
